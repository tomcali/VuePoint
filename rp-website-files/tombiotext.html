Tom works as a publisher, consultant, writer, and university instructor.  He is president and owner of Research Publishers LLC. Tom is the author of Research and Information Services: An Integrated Approach for Business (2007), Data and Text Mining: A Business Applications Approach (2005, Prentice Hall), coauthor of Marketing Research and Information Systems (2003), and co-editor of Qualitative Research Online (2004).

Tom holds graduate degrees in psychology (PhD, psychometrics) and statistics (MS) from the University of Minnesota and in business (MBA) and economics (MS) from the University of Oregon.  His undergraduate training was in the liberal arts at Ursinus College in Collegeville, Pennsylvania.  Tom also completed the editing certificate program at the University of Chicago and the professional publishing program at Stanford University.

Tom's research focus has been applied statistics and modeling. He has designed and conducted numerous empirical and simulation studies comparing traditional and data-adaptive methods, and he is regarded as an expert in the area of data mining.  His business consulting has covered a wide range of applications, including market segmentation, measurement, product design and positioning, pricing, sales forecasting, and retail site selection. .  Tom has consulted in these areas for more than thirty years.  Through Research Publishers LLC, he provides business research and modeling services under the Help2O name. 

Tom continues to teach economics, statistics, and research methods, working part-time for the University of Phoenix Online and Madison Area Technical College.  Previously, he was director of the A.C. Nielsen Center for Marketing Research.  He managed the Center's research and training program and taught in the School of Business at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Prior academic appointments were at the University of Oregon, Hamline University, and the University of Minnesota. Tom’s past employment also included technical, sales, and marketing positions for Hewlett-Packard and NCR Comten.

Tom is a member of the American Economics Association and the American Statistical Association.  He is a former Chair of the Statistics in Marketing Section of the American Statistical Association.  Tom is a also a member of the Board of Governors of the Interactive Marketing Research Organization within the Marketing Research Association.
